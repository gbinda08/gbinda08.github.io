Landing Page para uma loja de bijuteria.

About

The Junqueira Biju jewelry store does not have its own website, but it has a Facebook page where it promotes its products. The landing page was created to serve as a more direct and personalized point of contact for customers, as well as to offer a smoother and more enjoyable browsing experience. The design of the page is simple and elegant, reflecting the aesthetic of the store's products. It contains the owner's email and cell phone number.
